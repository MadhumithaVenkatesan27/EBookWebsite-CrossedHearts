Borrowing Your Textbook 175160

Art & Story: TaaRO·73 pages·4 Chapters·Release: April 2026

Afternoon Tea for Two

Story: Satsuki Haru · Illustrations: Fujimatsu Mei·37 pages·3 Chapters·Release: 2026

"If I were to die, who would you drink tea with?" Mitsuruko, refined yet endearingly absent-minded, opens her home to Ritsu, a practical vocational student newly arrived in Tokyo. What begins as a matter of convenience grows into a quiet companionship - shaped by shared laughter, small mishaps, and the comforting ritual of afternoon tea. A soft, heartwarming slice-of-life yuri about companionship, comfort, and finding the one person who turns a house into a home.
