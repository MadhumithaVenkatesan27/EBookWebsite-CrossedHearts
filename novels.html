When I opened my eyes, I was no longer myself. I had become the villainess of a zombie apocalypse - with a zombie's teeth buried in me. And yet… I didn't die. After being reborn into the world of a novel, Eunha Park refuses to die a second time as Raeliana McMillan, the side character fated to be poisoned by her fiancé. To change her destiny, she strikes a bold deal with the empire's most powerful and unpredictable man: Duke Noah Volstaire Wynknight.
