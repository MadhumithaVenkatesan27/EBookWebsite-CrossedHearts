Synopsis

Volume 2 of this public school romance where an honor student and a rebellious troublemaker—two complete opposites—find their feelings intensifying! At an elite boarding school, Noah, a model student, decides to keep his romantic feelings hidden so he can remain the closest friend of his roommate, Kai. One day, during a joint social party with students from another school, Noah suddenly falls ill. While Kai looks after him, Noah—dazed and half-asleep—accidentally reveals the feelings he has been trying to keep buried, murmuring, “I wish you were my boyfriend, Kai…” Meanwhile, Kai’s own feelings for Noah have been steadily growing stronger. Hearing Noah’s unguarded confession makes it impossible for him to hold back— And before he realizes it, he leans in and kisses him…!?
